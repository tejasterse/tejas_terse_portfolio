TEJAS TERSE

I'm a Photographer based in Kudal, Sindhudurg.

I focus on a diverse range of photography projects that explore the intricate relationship between humanity, nature, and culture. From capturing the essence of human emotions and individuality in portrait photography to documenting the vibrant energy and cultural significance of festivals around the world. In my landscape photography, I delve into the extraordinary forces impacting our environment, highlighting both the beauty of untouched nature. Additionally, my bird photography emphasizes the delicate balance of ecosystems, showcasing the grace and diversity of avian life.

Through my lens, I aim to raise awareness, evoke emotion, and inspire a deeper understanding of the world around us.
